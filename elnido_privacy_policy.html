Privacy Commitment

Welcome to El Nido Map – your trusted local guide to El Nido!

This privacy commitment applies to the El Nido Map application and any services we provide through it.

Your privacy matters to us. That’s why we want to be clear: our app does not collect any personal data from users.

Here are the key points of our privacy policy:

  • No personal data collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, address, email, or phone number.
  • No user tracking: We do not use tracking technologies like cookies to monitor your activity in the app.
  • Full access without personal info: You can explore all features of El Nido Map without needing to share any personal details.
  • Information security: While we don’t collect personal data, we are committed to safeguarding any information you may choose to share through direct contact (like feedback forms).

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about how we handle your information. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us.

Thank you for using El Nido Map and supporting local exploration!
